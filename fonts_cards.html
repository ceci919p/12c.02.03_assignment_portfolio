Fonts & Cards

This assignment was split into two. The first part focused on font hierarchy while the second focused on designing UI cards.

visit site ➔

Font hierarchy

The font hierarchy was made with consideration for legibility and aesthetics. For headlines I used “Abril Fatface” and for smaller headlines and body text I used “Work Sans”.

“Abril Fatface” is a display font that consists of titling weights and curves which are designed to catch the reader’s attention. With longer text this font doesn’t work. Therefore, I choose “Work Sans” in the weights optimized for on-screen text. Together they create contrast as one has serifs while the other does not.

UI Cards

The cards are designed using conventions and good practice. The layout of the cards is made using grid. The cards themselves and their content are placed using Flexbox. The media is placed on top to grab attention and a font hierarchy are made to guide the reader to the most important information. By using space, I have grouped name and title and separated them from the quote. The buttons at the bottom consists of two contrasting colors to differentiate the actions. Lastly hover effects are added to make the card more noticeable.
